Trip Overview

Embark on the fabled Andean Lakes Crossing, sailing from Argentina to Chile by catamaran. Explore the spectacular lakes, peaks, and waterfalls of Torres del Paine National Park. Hear from an expert about the Perito Moreno Glacier, then witness this magnificent ice mass in action. Discover the enormous penguin colonies of Martillo Island alongside a naturalist guide.
